ABOUT KRIYA

“INNOVATION MAY BE OTHERS PASSION BUT IT IS OUR PROFESSION!” Kriya – the brainchild of the Students Union – is the conglomeration of elite brains all across the Indian heartland. With a kaleidoscope of events covering all areas under the firmament, Kriya is the one stop to establish the student's prowess as the quintessential engineers. The biggest conflux of student's, industry and academia for thoroughly enriching experience, be it a video conference, guest lecture or workshop, encapsulates the epicenter of Technology for the ultimate salvation! The event now enjoys support from several firms and institutions and is recongnized by several others. It is ISO 9000 certified and has recieved UNESCO patronage. With events ranging from technical competitions to gaming showdowns to tests of skill and mettle, Kriya transcends simple technical events to become an inter-college extravaganza.Several events and workshops will take place during the 2-day fest. For a complete list, please see the appropriate page for each. Accomodation will also be provided, and status/availability can be checked on the Accomodations page.

PSG College of Technology, established in 1951 by that family of visionaries, is an institution par excellence. Rated among the top Private Engineering College in the country. PSG Tech offers its students unmatched scope and avenues into the field of Engineering.

The college today has student strength of over 7000 studying in about 23 Departments. With an able team of experienced faculty, the college produces a talented set of engineers year after year. The college gives a lot of emphasis on research and development. PSG College of technology also has collaborations with top Engineering Industries around the world to give its students hands-on training and experience. The college has hosted several International conferences and Symposiums and has been visited by the who's who of the today's world. As an institution ranked no.1 in Industry-Institute Interface in India, consecutively for the last 3 years (2013, 2014 & 2015); the college is one of the largest sources of promoting entrepreneurship in the country.

The PSG group, in addition to the engineering colleges, also encompasses several other institutions including Medical, Management, Arts & Science colleges and primary and secondary education centres striving to provide a wholesome education to the society.
