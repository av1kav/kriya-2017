Workshop Description

The beauty of natyre reflected in art is just another reminder of our need to protect it. This workshop uses corn husk, which is the outer covering of corn, to create beautiful works of art and fashion such as husk flowers, decors, husk dolls, wreaths etc. These are versatile, inexpensive, easy to work with and produce a wide variety of classic, long-lasting decorations. Students will be taught to make these artworks.


Highlights

  • Eco-friendly crafts.
  • Easy to work with, DIY crafts
  • Participants will be able to create flowers, dolls, wreaths and decors from corn husk
  • They will learn a new way of creating Fashin Products

Fee Details
