Workshop Description

The workshop aims at educating aspiring engineers on the additive manufacturing techniques of Rapid Prototyping. It is an opportunity for the students to enrich their knowledge on techniques such as Stereolithography, Fusion Deposition Moulding and Selective Laser Sintering. The workshop is being conducted in association with PSG TECH TIFAC-CORE which has a full-fledged Rapid Prototyping facility and is extending its bureau of services for industries from the inception in Rapid Prototyping, Innovative Re-engineering and Rapid Tooling.
