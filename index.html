INNOVATION MAY BE OTHERS PASSION BUT IT IS OUR PROFESSION!

Kriya – the brainchild of the Students Union – is the conglomeration of elite brains all across the Indian heartland. With a kaleidoscope of events covering all areas under the firmament, Kriya is the one stop to establish the student's prowess as the quintessential engineers. The biggest conflux of student's, industry and academia for thoroughly enriching experience, be it a video conference, guest lecture or workshop, encapsulates the epicenter of Technology for the ultimate salvation!

PRESTIGIOUS PSG

PSG COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY, established in 1951 by that family of visionaries, is an institution par excellence. Rated among the top Private Engineering College in the country. PSG Tech offers its students unmatched scope and avenues into the field of Engineering. The college today has student strength of over 8000 studying in about 23 Departments. With an able team of experienced faculty, the college produces a talented set of engineers year after year. The college gives a lot of emphasis on research and development The College has hosted several International conferences and Symposiums and has been visited by the who's who of the today's world.

A BRAND NEW WEBPAGE

Over the years, as the technical symposium of Kriya has attracted larger and larger crowds, it became clear that our website needed to scale to accomodate the vast number of users. This new website is built specifically for ease of use, and caters to a wide variety of display sizes, from TV screens to laptops to mobile devices. The page is also the first to be entirely engineered by our very own student team, meaning that content is bloat-free, optimized and responsive.
