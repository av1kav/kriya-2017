ACCOMODATION

The college today has student strength of over 7000 studying in about 23 Departments. With an able team of experienced faculty, the college produces a talented set of engineers year after year. The college gives a lot of emphasis on research and development. PSG College of technology also has collaborations with top Engineering Industries around the world to give its students hands-on training and experience. The college has hosted several International conferences and Symposiums and has been visited by the who's who of the today's world. As an institution ranked no.1 in Industry-Institute Interface in India, consecutively for the last 3 years (2013, 2014 & 2015); the college is one of the largest sources of promoting entrepreneurship in the country.

The PSG group, in addition to the engineering colleges, also encompasses several other institutions including Medical, Management, Arts & Science colleges and primary and secondary education centres striving to provide a wholesome education to the society.
