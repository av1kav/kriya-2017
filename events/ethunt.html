Event Description

E-treasure hunt is one of many different types of games with one or more players who try to find hidden treasure by following a series of clues. It tests the ability to solve problems and improves the team building skills. To make it more interactive and intresting, the treasure hunt game is hosted on a website. Put on your ddetective glasses and hunt for the clues.!
