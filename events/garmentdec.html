Garment Deconstruction

Association:

Fashion Technology Association


Contact Information

Swaathi Sree J
Mob: 9865153610


Event Description

Garment deconstruction is all about converting old garments into fashionable new ones. Participants will get to discover their own creativeness, which will be showcased in the new styles they create. Gives an idea on how to make good usem of old garments, that will otherwise be useless.


Event Venue

Quadrangle


Rules

  • Garment will be provided
  • Participants should bring basic requirements such as scissors, thread, needles etc.
  • There should only be 3 participants in a team.
  • Only 10 groups will be selected for the final round.
  • Maximum time required for the event: 120 minutes (including both rounds)

Round 1: Connexions

2 or more pictures that depicts a work or a phrase when they are connected together will be provided. Students will have to find out the word/phrase from the gives set of pictures. Pictures will be based on common brands, slogans, logos, etc. 10-15 questions will be given to the participants.

  • 3 students in a group
  • Time duration for each question: 1 minute
  • 10 groups will be selected for the final round


Round 2: Garment Deconstruction

New styles emerge from creative thinking. In this event, participants will have to deconstruct old garments into fashionable ones. They can cut/fold/snip/tie/stitch the given garment to create a new style in the end. The new grament should be displayed on a mannequin. The group that creates the best outfit will win this round.

  • Groups that have been selected in the prelims will participate in this round.
  • Three students per group
  • Max. time: 90 minutes
