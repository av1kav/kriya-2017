Event Description

This event is a brief presentation of their innovative technical ideas. The teams will be selected based on the abstracts and the novelty.
Related topics:

  • Wearable Sensorics
  • Digital signal and image processing
  • Emerging Technologies in Mobile and Wireless Communication


Event Venue

Quadrangle


Rules

  • Maximum of two authors per team.
  • Abstract should be mailed to eceapsg@gmail.com (in a word document-.docx) for not more than 300 words before due date (8-2-2017).
  • The confirmation mail will be sent to the selected participants a week before the event.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform the participants of the same. Violation of any rule can result in rejection of paper.
  • The oral presentation should be in form of Microsoft PowerPoint slides(.pptx) which contain the brief of the paper not to exceed 21.
  • The presentation should be brief and not more than 10 minutes.
  • Participants are requested to bring two hardcopies of their paper.
  • The selected participants should submit a hard copy of paper with abstract before commencement of the event in A4 sheets not exceeding ten pages.
  • The hardcopy of the paper should be in IEEE format.
  • Participation certificates will be provided.
