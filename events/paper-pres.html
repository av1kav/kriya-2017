Event Description

Garment deconstruction is all about converting old garments into fashionable new ones. Participants will get to discover their own creativeness, which will be showcased in the new styles they create. Gives an idea on how to make good usem of old garments, that will otherwise be useless.


Event Venue

Quadrangle


Rules

  • Each team should consist of 2 members
  • Abstract of about 1 or 2 pages should be sent by mail to sec.cea2016.psgtech@gmail.com within 23.2.2017.
  • Abstract must be brief, precise and should be in IEEE format.
  • Candidates must include their name, college name and contact details with the abstract.
  • Teams will be selected based on their abstract.
  • Selected candidates will be intimated by mail.
  • Each team should present their paper for 5 minutes and queries will be asked for 5 minutes.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Round 1: Connexions

Candidates should present their topics in brief within 5 minutes and queries will be asked for 5 minutes in their presentation.
The topics for presentation are as follows:

  • Green building
  • Repair and Rehabilitation of structures
  • Sustainable development in construction industry
  • Automation in construction field
  • Nano technology in civil Engineering
  • Structural seismic safety and fragility analysis
  • Under water construction
  • Environmental impacts due to construction and remedial measures
  • Metro projects
  • Intelligent transport system
  • Innovative technologies in Sky scrappers
  • Case study on a hydroelectric project
  • Bacterial concrete
  • Sky bus technology
  • Case study on air pollution in New Delhi.
