Event Description

The event majorly focuses on the importance of cleanliness and the need for much more pro-active and innovative ideas which enriches one of our country’s current missions “Swachh Bharat”. We will be projecting photos/mentioning more places to the contestants for which they have to plan and give their course of action to clean the place efficiently and its future maintenance as well.


Event Venue

Quadrangle


Rules

  • Maximum of 2 members in a group is permitted.
  • Time allotted for each group is 15 minutes.
  • Judges decision is final.

Round 1: Connexions

Picture revealing different types of pollution will be displayed on the Kriya website. The team that participates should submit their detailed description to control the pollutions and to dispose the waste efficiently, before the deadline to mail id. The best ideas will be short-listed for the next round.


Round 2: Garment Deconstruction

Short-listed teams will be intimated through mail along with the topics of project proposal which would be for the final round in the Kriya event.
