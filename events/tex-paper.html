Event Description

This event is a platform to expose the talent and in- depth knowledge on an interested areas.

  • Advances in non woven
  • Industrial Textile
  • Filtration Textile
  • Acoustic Textile
  • Textile Composite
  • 3D Weaving
The event will be held in two phases.


Event Venue

Quadrangle


Rules

  • A team of maximum two can participate
  • Electronic gadgets should not be used in the competition
  • The competition is open to all UG students

Phase 1

The participants are expected to submit their technical paper in the .doc format to the respective E-mail ID. The selected participants will be informed via e-mail.


Phase 2

Three hard copies should be submitted before presentation. During the main event, the contestants will get 7 minutes to present their paper, at the end of which there will be query session. The presentation should be in .ppt format.
